Shipping Guide

How to Package Your Cards

  1. Use a sturdy box or padded envelope
  2. Wrap cards in bubble wrap or protective sleeves
  3. Place cards in a secure container within the package
  4. Add padding to prevent movement during shipping
  5. Seal the package securely with tape

Shipping Instructions

  • Use the provided prepaid shipping label
  • Do not write on the package
  • Drop off at any authorized shipping location
  • Keep your tracking number for reference

Important Notes

  • We recommend insuring your package
  • Take a photo of your packaged cards before shipping
  • Contact us immediately if there are any issues
