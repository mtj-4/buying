Shipping Guide

1

Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary supplies:

  • Submission form (page 1 of shipping label)
  • Bubble wrap
  • Packing tape
2

Wrap and Box Your Wax

Follow these steps to properly package your wax:

  • Bubble wrap each box either individually or in groups
  • Place it in your cardboard box in a way that limits movement

üí° Pro Tip

Take photos of your wax boxes before shipping for your records. This helps with insurance claims if needed.

3

Include Your Submission Form and Seal the Box

Complete the packaging process:

  • Place the submission form on top of the wax
  • Seal the box using packing tape
4

Apply the Tracking Label

Attach your shipping label properly:

  • Cut or fold the label so that only the part of the page with the label on it is showing
  • Tape or affix the label to the package so that it is unobstructed
  • If you mess up, feel free to reprint the label and do it again
5

Drop the Package Off

Complete the shipping process:

  • Go to any local USPS and drop the package off
